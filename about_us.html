About Jai Hari Kitchen

We, at Jai Hari Kitchen began our operations into modular kitchen designing, manufacturing and exporting in the year 2005, under the leadership of our mentor Mr. Bagul, who has rich experience in the field of interior decoration. Under his guidance, the company has seen steep success, catering diversified clientele, setting new benchmarks for quality and style. We have ensured everything needed to offer modular kitchen designs and accessories of matchless quality with unique styling, as part of our world class infrastructure.

Our Team

A team of adroit professionals have been recruited by our management after rigorous rounds of interviews. These professionals have the ability to meet all the organizational goals within the specified time-frame. Our team members are highly qualified, committed & industrious and utilize their in-depth domain experience in manufacturing kitchen appliances and wooden furniture. It is by virtue of the unwavering efforts of these professionals that our organization has been able to occupy an enviable status in the market. Our team comprises: Procurement agents, Production engineers, Craftsmen, Quality associates, Warehousing & logistics personnel, Administration staff, Marketing executives, Skilled workers.

Our Assurance Quality

Our organization has earned immense appreciation in the market for consistently maintaining the quality of each product. The products we provide are manufactured using quality approved raw material obtained from the most authentic and certified market vendors. We are facilitated with an advanced quality testing unit, which enables us to thoroughly check the finished products on well-defined parameters. Furthermore, we have also appointed adroit quality controllers, who make sure to test each product on the basis of the following parameters : Rusting, Tensile strength, Polishing and finishing, Painting, Design accuracy, Material strength.

Client Satisfaction

Being a client-based business enterprise, we strive hard to manufacture a qualitative range of kitchen appliances and wooden furniture or our prestigious clients. To maintain the satisfaction level of our clients, we make sure to provide them with the products manufactured in accordance to their detailed specifications and dimensions. Our team members follow ethical business practices and maintain transparencies in all dealings. Furthermore, the implementation of quality focus approach, client-centric policies best-in-class product line, we have been successful in acquiring a huge base of satisfied clients across the territories of domestic market.
